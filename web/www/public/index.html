Sentry Watch

Always on Guard

Concept

Our smart, wearable emergency response device automatically detects signs of struggle and alerts specified emergency contacts and services of the user's situation and location. Whether it be walking home at night or wandering the streets of an unfamiliar part of the city, with this device, users can be rest assured that they will always have a sentry watching over them.

Features

Our device analyzes raw accelerometer data in real-time to determine whether the wearer is in any kind of trouble. Our algorithm, specially optimized and implemented for the incredibly lightweight hardware, fits the data as closely as possible to optimal curves and calculate the rhythm, jitter, and jerkiness to approximate a running derivative. If they surpass a calibrated threshold, a notification is sent to our server to contact the user's emergency contact (and optionally emergency services) with their location.
The device also features a discrete panic button that lets a user notify their emergency contact (or an emergency service) if they are in distress, but can't afford to run to fight back.
